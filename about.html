My Education. A Journey from Musician to Young Professional

Originally starting college as a music education major, my journey to where I am today has been nothing short of interesting.

I attended the University of Houston from Fall 2018 - December 2021. Within that time, I studied theory, marched in a myriad of football games, became a member of Sigma Alpha Iota Music Fraternity, and was a flute teacher to many students across various schools.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, my gears shifted and I began becoming more interested in business. I transitioned to a Bachelor of Arts in Music & tacked on a Business Minor.

Since then, I have graduated and continue to grow in my business leadership skills and learn about business functionality and technology.

I Climb to My Goals. Literally.

Ever since I was a child, fitness has always been a crucial part of my life and hobbies. My first love of sports came from Tae-Kwon-Do. I practiced for about 7 years as a child into my teens, finally ranking up to a black belt.

Although continuing fitness in other ways, within the past year I have taken on a new adventure: climbing.

I now continue to boulder and top rope climb with my friends as often as I can. Always pushing myself as well as the others around me.

Next climbing goal? Climb outside! Stay tuned...

Cheers to Adventure. And those that tag along with you.

I am also no stranger to solo travel, hiking, and National Parks. Ask me about my favorite hikes!
